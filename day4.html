SA Care Tokyo Paralympics Instagram Post 1

I created a series of Instagram posts for SA Care, a disability support company based in Adelaide, South Australia. These posts were meant to inform their subscribers about the schedule and events of the Tokyo Paralympics Day 4. Since SA Care values keeping their community updated, these posts were essential.

Technical Elements:

  • Custom Templates:To design these Instagram posts, I didn't use pre-made templates. Instead, I created custom templates that perfectly matched SA Care's brand and values. This allowed the posts to look unique and aligned with the company's identity.
  • Multiple Design Elements: Each post was carefully designed using various design elements. This included selecting specific colors, fonts, and graphics that represent SA Care's values and mission. The choice of these elements was critical in conveying the right message and engaging the audience.

Outcome:

The SA Care Tokyo Paralympics Day 4 Instagram Posts were a successful project that effectively communicated important information to the audience. By creating custom templates and using a combination of design elements, the posts stood out and captured the essence of SA Care's values.

These posts not only served the purpose of informing the audience about the Paralympics but also reinforced SA Care's commitment to keeping their community updated and engaged. The project demonstrated the power of thoughtful design in conveying important messages and maintaining a strong brand presence on social media.
